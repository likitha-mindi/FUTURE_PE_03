# PROMPT ENGINEERING EXPLANATION 
## Objective
To create SEO-optimized blog content and structured content clusters for a digital marketing agency.
## Prompt Strategy
1. Role Assignment
The AI was assigned the role of "SEO content strategist" to ensure structured and search-focused output.

2. Clear SEO Requirements
Prompts included:
- H1-H3 structure
- Meta description
- Target keywords
- FAQ section
This ensures search optimization.

3. Local SEO Adaptation
Hyderabad location was specified to generate geo-targeted content.

4. Content Clustering
Internal linking ideas help improve website authority and ranking potential.

5. Conversion Focus
CTA was added to convert readers into leads.
## Outcome
The AI generated:
- Structured SEO outline
- Business-focused blog content
- Internal linking strategy
- Localized keyword integration
